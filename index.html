Founder

Cinthya Garcia

Peruvian-born · Napa Valley, California

Cinthya Garcia is a wellness curator and founder of Kaia — a platform creating elevated, transformative experiences across California and beyond.

Based in Napa Valley, she blends her cultural roots, love for sensory rituals, and eye for aesthetics to design spaces that invite presence, connection, and expansion.
